Welcome to the **Geospatial Observation and Applications Lab (GOAL)** at CUJ.

Our research focuses on:
- Remote Sensing & GIS Applications
- Hydrological & Climate Modeling
- Drought Monitoring & Prediction
- Land Use and Land Cover Change

We aim to bridge the gap between geospatial science and environmental sustainability.
