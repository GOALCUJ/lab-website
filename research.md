# Research Themes

Our research focuses on the following areas:

## 1. Hydroclimatic Extremes
- Analysis of **flash droughts** and their socio-environmental impacts.
- Understanding the role of **atmospheric rivers** in drought recovery.

## 2. Remote Sensing & GIS
- Application of **SAR and optical remote sensing** for hydrological studies.
- Development of GIS-based models for **flood and drought risk assessment**.

## 3. Climate Change & Water Resources
- Investigating the **effects of reservoirs and land use change** on droughts.
- Developing climate adaptation strategies for **sustainable water management**.
