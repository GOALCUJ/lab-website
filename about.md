# About GOAL

The **Geospatial Observations and Application Lab (GOAL)** is led by Dr. Shanti Shwarup Mahto, an Assistant Professor at the Central University of Jharkhand, Ranchi. The lab explores various aspects of climate science, hydrology, and geospatial technologies.

Our mission is to:
- Enhance understanding of hydroclimatic extremes.
- Develop geospatial tools for environmental applications.
- Collaborate with researchers globally to address climate challenges.

For collaboration inquiries, please reach out via our [contact page](/contact/).
